Ask questions about your TELUS analytics data

Hello! I'm your Analytics Assistant. You can ask me questions about your TELUS sales rep data.

Try questions like:

  • Which sales rep has the most visits?
  • Show me the top 5 sales reps by credit card additions
  • What are the metrics for boutique telus?

You can also ask for calculations and statistics:

  • What is the ratio of credit card additions to visits for each sales rep?
  • Show me the top 5 sales reps with the highest ratio of credit card additions to visits
  • Calculate the ratio of order confirmations to visits
  • What is the average number of credit card additions across all reps?
  • Is there a correlation between visits and credit card additions?
